Dear Editors,

Please consider our manuscript entitled "Lightweight Tamper-Evident IoT Log Integrity Verification with Merkle Proofs: Implementation, Validation, and Benchmarking" for publication in Computers & Security.

The paper presents a practical integrity-verification pipeline for IoT audit logs based on Merkle-tree commitments and inclusion proofs. Beyond proposing the design, we report an implementation audit that identifies and corrects evaluation defects (tampering metric miscounting and repeated full-tree rebuilds) that materially affect conclusions. We then provide reproducible benchmarks and a literature-grounded discussion of trust and deployment trade-offs versus blockchain-based audit logging.

The manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under consideration by another journal.
